I'M CINDY CHEN AND I'M A GRAPHIC AND WEB DESIGNER FROM TORONTO.


I've worked for more than 4 years as a graphic and website designer after having graduated from Vanier College in 2007 with a focus on Document Design and Multimedia Technology.

I'm a down-to-earth, forward-thinking creative professional with a passion for design. I not only design websites, landing pages, but also create print communications like branding/logos, newsletters, banners, advertising and promotional posters. I believe unique user focused designs are built on a responsive foundation with clean HTML and CSS code. I have an eye on details and I am very keen on typography and color theory. I am 70% designer and 30% coder.
